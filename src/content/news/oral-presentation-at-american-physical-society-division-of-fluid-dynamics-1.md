## Presentation Highlights

- Presented an experimental investigation of counter-rotating vortex pair interactions with a deformable free surface.
- Discussed the experimental methodology, instrumentation design, and key findings related to the upwelling and downwelling regions generated by the vortex pair.
- Engaged with researchers working in turbulence, multiphase flows, free-surface flows, and vortex dynamics.
