## Presentation Highlights

- Presented experimental investigation of counter-rotating vortex interaction with a deformable free surface  
- Discussed experimental methodology and instrumentation design  
- Engaged in technical discussions on vortex-induced surface deformation  
- Networked with researchers working in turbulence, multiphase flow, and vortex dynamics  

## Conference Experience

Beyond the presentation, attending APS DFD allowed me to:

- Explore new research in turbulence, instability, and multiphase flow  
- Attend invited talks by leading fluid mechanicians  
- Connect with graduate researchers and faculty across institutions  
- Gain perspective on current research trends in fluid mechanics
